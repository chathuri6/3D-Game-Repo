Lego Maze 
_________

Player must find the girl kidnapped by the enemies by going throhg several mazees and collecting door keys with in allocated time.
Each level is unlocked when played collect the door key on levels.
To win player must find a safe way to reach the girl through the maze in the final level.

Each coin collected by the player adds 5 bonous points to the total score.
Each door key holds 10 points.
Reaching to the girl passes 100 points to the player score.

Can load earlier saved level.
When player finishes the game can replay to achive better scores.
